# ⚙️ Configuration
### ✨ Toggle Particles / Sound Effects
### 🔥 Toggle Lava Walker
### ⭕ Choose Radius for Lava Walker
### ⌛ Choose Duration for each phase of Lava Walker

****
### With these options:
- Players who only like the visuals on vanilla Frost Walker can also enjoy it!
- Players who cannot afford too many effects can still enjoy the Lava Walker mechanics.
- Server operators can tailor the mechanic to their needs.
****
_(note: this mod does **not** add levels to Frost Walker! But it is compatible with any mod that might)_


- Allows to walk on Lava with the vanilla **Frost Walker** enchantment.
- Adds nice visual and sound effects to the lava to blackstone conversion.
- As well as to the vanilla water to ice conversion.
- Works with any level of Frost Walker!

### Vanilla Design
✦ By default, the Lava Radius is smaller than the Ice Radius by 1.

✦ Blocks revert faster to Lava than Ice to Water to remain challenging in the Nether.

****

### Note:
- Blocks can be mined but drop nothing (like Ice).
- Blocks affected will revert to lava even if the client / server crashes, so your world is safe!
- Required on both client & server.
- Compatible with Modmenu
- No dependencies, enjoy!
